# Problem Statement

## 1. Business Context
The company operates a subscription-based digital service in a competitive global market.
Customer acquisition costs are increasing, while retention directly impacts long-term revenue and profitability.

## 2. Stakeholder & Decision
Primary stakeholder: Head of Growth / Product Manager  
Decision to support: 
Which customers should be prioritized for retention interventions in the next quarter?

## 3. Core Problem
The company lacks a data-driven method to identify customers at high risk of churn before they leave.

## 4. Why This Problem Matters
Reducing churn by even 1â€“2% can significantly increase lifetime value and stabilize revenue forecasts.

## 5. Success Metric
Primary metric: Churn prediction accuracy (Recall on churned customers)  
Business metric: Reduction in monthly churn rate

## 6. Constraints & Assumptions
- Limited historical customer behavior data
- No real-time streaming data
- Intervention budget is limited

## 7. Expected Impact
A ranked list of high-risk customers to enable targeted retention strategies.
